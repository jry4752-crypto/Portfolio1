About Me

The Person Behind the Work

Free
Elec 1
2026

Hey, I'm Paul! 👋

I'm a Filipino student and aspiring creative designer with a passion for visual communication, digital art, and motion design. Currently enrolled in Free Elec 1 (Introduction to Graphic Design), I've been sharpening my eye for design and exploring the tools that turn ideas into visual experiences.

My journey into design started with curiosity — wondering how the visuals we see every day are made, and what makes some designs truly stand out. Through this course, I've worked on posters, infographics, logo design, and animation, each project pushing my skills further.

Beyond the classroom, I'm someone who appreciates storytelling through imagery, believes in the power of clean, intentional design, and is always looking to grow — whether that's through new tools, new styles, or new challenges.

Adobe Photoshop Adobe Illustrator Adobe Animate Canva Typography Logo Design Motion Graphics Infographic Design

"My creative vision is to design with purpose — to create visuals that don't just look good, but say something meaningful. I want to use design as a bridge between ideas and people."
